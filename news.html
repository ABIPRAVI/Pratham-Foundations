Our Educational Service

During the lockdown period in India, schools were shut down and children couldn't continue their education as they didn't have a smartphone but we provided 2000 tablet phones freely to the children so that they can study well.

Our Services during Disasters:

During the 2018 flood in Kerala we collected donations from all possible resources and gave them to Kerala. We also provided food and shelter to the people.

The 2015 flood in South India damaged Chennai massively, during that time we helped the people by providing them food, blankets, and nearly 1000 people stayed in our home for 10 days.

The 2013 flood in Uttarakhand is recorded to be the worst massive disaster. Even during that time, we helped many needy and poor people
