Introduction :

  • Pratham trust mainly focuses on helping the poor and needy by providing them a proper basic requirements which includes food, clothing and free medical camps.
  • Charity is the act of extending love and kindness to others unconditionally, which is a conscious act but the decision is made by the heart and soul without expecting a reward.
  • Our trust family is staffed by volunteers dedicated to improve the lives of abandoned poor children, adults, orphans, and homeless in our nation.

The sole aim of Pratham trust is to eradicate poverty and hunger from India
